q: How can I do mobile recharge?
a: To do a mobile recharge using the bKash merchant app:

1. From the home screen, tap the "Mobile Recharge" feature.
2. Enter the mobile number you want to recharge.
3. Select the mobile operator. If you have recharged this number before, you can tap the operator icon above the amount input screen to change the operator.
4. Enter the recharge amount.
5. If available, view any special offers by tapping the "My Offer" tab after entering the amount.
6. Tap "Proceed" to continue.
7. Confirm the details and enter your bKash PIN to complete the recharge.
8. You will receive an in-app notification confirming the successful recharge.

If you face any issues or lack the required permission, contact your nearest bKash representative.

q: How can I view daily/monthly sales reports?
a: You can view daily and monthly sales reports from the bKash merchant app by following these steps:

- For daily summary:
  1. From the home page of the merchant app, tap on "Transactions."
  2. Tap on "Transaction Summary."
  3. Select the "Daily Summary" tab.
  4. View the summary for today’s date or select a specific date from the calendar to see that day’s summary.
  5. You can view summaries for up to the last 30 days including today.
  6. The daily summary includes starting and ending balance, total number and amount of debit and credit transactions.
  7. Filters like "in," "out," and "fee" can be applied to see specific transaction details.

- For monthly summary:
  1. From the home page, tap on "Transactions."
  2. Tap on "Transaction Summary."
  3. Select the "Monthly Summary" tab.
  4. View the summary of the current month or select from up to 6 month cards.
  5. The monthly summary shows starting and ending balance, total number and amount of debit and credit transactions.
  6. Filters like "in," "out," and "fee" can be applied for detailed views.

You can also check the latest update time for both daily and monthly summaries under the "Last Updated" option.

q: How can I solve the problem if a payment fails?
a: If a payment fails, no money will be deducted from your merchant account. You will see a message indicating the failure. Additionally, the customer will be notified by SMS about the failed payment. If the amount was deducted despite the failure, it will be refunded to your merchant account within 3 working days. You can also check the latest transaction details in the app to confirm the status. If issues persist, please contact your nearest bKash representative for assistance.

q: Can I transfer money from my bKash Merchant Account to a bank account?
a: No, currently you cannot transfer money directly from your bKash Merchant Account to a bank account. However, you can transfer money from your merchant account to your own Visa debit card using the "bKash to card" feature in the merchant app. This service is available for Visa debit cards only, and a 1.39% charge applies per transaction. Transfers may take up to 30 minutes if fast fund is enabled on your card, otherwise up to 3 working days. For issues with transfers, contact your card-issuing bank or file a complaint with bKash if unresolved.
